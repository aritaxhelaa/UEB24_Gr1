Vegan Buddha Bowl Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp tahini

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa and chickpeas.
  2. Arrange all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Vegan Tacos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Instructions:

  1. Heat tortillas and fill with beans, corn, and avocado.
  2. Top with salsa and serve.

Vegan Pasta Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olives
  • 1/2 cup red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta and let cool.
  2. Mix with veggies and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Serve chilled.

Vegan Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Serve chilled.

Vegan Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry tofu and veggies in sesame oil.
  2. Add soy sauce and cook until tender.
  3. Serve with rice or noodles.
